Shine Gala.

The Shine Gala is a dazzling Victoria evening of fashion, food and fundraising, all in support of BC Children's Hospital. In 2024 we were honoured to join in and help tell the stories at its heart.

Volare Media came on as media sponsor, donating all of our time and resources to make these films, because the families behind them deserve to be seen.

Volare Media · Media Sponsor · 2024

2024. Media Sponsor · Two films

For the 2024 gala we produced two films. The first shares Quinn's story through the voices of her family, from the moment they learned she had cancer to the journey that followed. The second follows Quinn's Beads of Courage, the program that marks every brave step of a child's treatment.
