Why we do it

people to give.

Our story — Community

Years of stories
worth giving to

Volare Media · Community & giving

Of all the work we're lucky enough to do, this is the work that stays with us. Some of our proudest films were never made to sell a thing — they were made to help a cause be felt. Here's how that became such a big part of who Volare Media is.

How it started

It started with a call from Peninsula Gives. They had a simple, powerful idea: bring real media into their fundraising events — films and videos guests could actually watch, so the cause wasn't just described to a room, it was felt in it.

They were right. Video does something a microphone and a podium can't. With visuals and sound working together, it reaches past the facts and into the emotion — and for a cause like theirs, that emotion is the whole point. Peninsula Gives raises funds in support of BC Children's Hospital, and we couldn't think of a better use of our cameras. We signed on as their media sponsor for 2023 — and we've been proud to come back in 2024 and 2025.

Bringing it to the Shine Gala

In 2024 we had the opportunity to work with the Shine Gala — a glamorous Victoria evening that also raises money for BC Children's Hospital. More great stories, the same mission: helping the kids and families who depend on world-class care get the support they need.

Tailgate Toolkit: mental health on the job site

Not every cause we care about is a hospital. The Tailgate Toolkit is a mental-health initiative built for BC's construction community — an industry that loses far too many of its own to a crisis that too often goes unspoken. We made the campaign that carried its message onto 61 radio and TV stations across the province, and credited the whole crew along the way. It's some of the most important work we've ever pointed a camera at. Read the full story.

Standing with the Women's Transition House

In 2024 we also produced a film for Fashion Splash, a fundraiser for the Victoria Women's Transition House — the organization that gives women and children a safe place to go when they're leaving violence and abuse. A very different cause, the same belief: the right story, told well, moves people to give.

Why it matters to us

We build marketing videos for a living, and we're good at it. But work like this is a reminder of why we picked up a camera in the first place — to tell stories worth sharing. Helping a hospital, a charity or a fundraiser be seen and supported isn't a side project for us. It's part of who Volare Media is, and it always will be.

If you're a nonprofit, charity or fundraiser with a story to tell, we'd genuinely love to hear from you.
