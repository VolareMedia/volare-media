Community & giving

The causes
we love.

Some of the work that means the most to us never sold a single product. Volare Media loves partnering with nonprofits, charities and fundraisers, telling the stories that raise money, raise awareness and genuinely fill our hearts.

Why we do it

Video moves
people to give.

Video has a way of reaching people that a microphone and a podium can't. Visuals and sound together get past the facts and into the emotion, and for a good cause, that emotion is the whole point. That's why we bring the exact same film craft to a fundraiser that we bring to any commercial client. Same crew, same standard, same care. It's some of the most rewarding work we get to do.

Also close to our hearts

The Tailgate
Toolkit.

Read the story

Not every cause we care about is a gala. The Tailgate Toolkit is a mental-health initiative for BC's construction community, an industry that loses far too many of its own to a crisis that too often goes unspoken. We made the campaign that carried its message onto 61 radio and TV stations across the province. It's some of the most important work we've ever pointed a camera at.
