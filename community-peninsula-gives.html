Peninsula Gives.

Peninsula Gives is a local charity that throws unforgettable events to raise money for BC Children's Hospital. They were the ones who first brought us into this world, and every year since, we've been proud to help their story reach further than any room ever could.

On every one of these, Volare Media was the media sponsor. We donated all of our time and resources to make these films happen, because we believe in what Peninsula Gives does.
