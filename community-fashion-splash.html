Fashion Splash.

Fashion Splash is a Victoria fashion showcase with a purpose: every year it raises money for the Victoria Women's Transition House, the organization that gives women and children a safe place to go when they're leaving violence and abuse.

For the 2024 event we came on as a media sponsor, donating our time and resources to produce a film that put the cause front and centre.

Volare Media · Media Sponsor · 2024

2024. Media Sponsor · Fundraiser film

The 2024 Fashion Splash gathered Vancouver Island's designers, models and beauty professionals for a night in support of the Victoria Women's Transition House, marking the organization's 50th year. We produced the film that carried its message to the room.
