About

Crew-first.
Story-first.

We're Volare Media — a video production company based on Vancouver Island, working across British Columbia and Canada. Marketing videos that perform are the core of what we do, and we bring the same film craft to documentaries, music videos and film.

Crew-first

Better sets.
Better film.

Volare Media is a crew-first production company. That means clear roles, fair treatment and a thoughtful crew structure on every shoot. It's the right way to work — and it's also why our footage looks the way it does. People do their best work when the set runs well.
