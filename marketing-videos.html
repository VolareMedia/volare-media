Why it works

Pretty isn't the point.
Performing is.

Strategy-led

Built around the goal

We start with the audience and the result you need, then design the video to move them.

A beautiful video that doesn't sell anything is an expensive art project. Before we talk shots, we get clear on who you're trying to reach, what you want them to do, and where they'll see it. Every creative choice ladders back to that.

It starts with our creative direction: concept and script first.

Film craft

Cinema-grade, marketing-smart

Real camera, lighting, sound and colour craft, in service of a message that performs.

We come from the film world, so your ad gets the same care as a broadcast commercial: proper lensing, lighting, sound design and colour grade. That polish is what makes a brand look as good as it actually is, and it's the difference between a video people skip and one they finish.

Every platform

Cut for where it lives

Wide for TV and YouTube, square and vertical for social, each cut on purpose.

A 60-second hero film is just the start. We plan cutdowns and vertical versions from the beginning, so one shoot delivers a TV spot, YouTube pre-roll, Instagram reels and TikToks, each framed and paced for its feed rather than cropped as an afterthought.

Seen widely

Great work, actually seen

We think about reach and distribution, not just the edit. A film matters most when it's seen.

The most thoughtful commercial in the world does nothing sitting on a hard drive. We help you think through placement and reach, the same way our Tailgate Toolkit campaign ran for a full year on 61 stations across BC.

Common questions

Asked, answered.

Budget

What does it cost?

It depends on scope. The honest answer starts with your goal.

A single social ad and a full TV campaign are very different budgets. We scope every project before you commit, and we break down where the money goes in our cost guide.

Scale

One video or a campaign?

Both. One shoot can feed an entire campaign.

Many clients start with one hero commercial or brand film, then we cut it into shorter ads, vertical social versions and teasers, so a single production run works across TV, YouTube and paid social.

Concept

Do you write it?

Yes. Positioning, concept and script come first.

Most projects start with our creative direction, so the idea is right before we roll. Bring a brief or a blank page, either works. See creative direction.

Social

Vertical for social?

Always, in every aspect ratio your channels need.

We finish wide for YouTube and TV, and square and vertical for Instagram, TikTok and paid social, each version cut specifically for its placement rather than cropped after the fact.
