Turnaround

Brief in, boards back

Send the brief; get treatments, references and honest costs back fast — before your client meeting, not after it.

Agency timelines don't wait, so neither do we. When a brief lands, you get a treatment with visual references and a straight answer on what it costs to pull off — usually with more than one direction so you can walk into the client meeting with options.

And because we come from film sets, the schedule you approve is the schedule that happens. Calm shoot days, no surprise overages, no drama the week of delivery.

Broadcast

Cleared for air

Broadcast specs, clearance and station delivery handled — one of our spots ran on 61 stations across BC for a year.

Getting a spot made is half the job; getting it on air is the other half. We deliver to broadcast spec for TV and radio and handle the clearance paperwork most production companies hand back to the agency.

The proof: a single Volare Media commercial for BC's construction community cleared onto 61 radio and TV stations across the province and ran for a full year. Read that story

Campaign

One shoot, every asset

Hero film, 15s and 30s cutdowns, pre-roll and verticals — planned before we roll, so the media plan never runs dry.

We plan the shoot around your media plan, not the other way round. Every deliverable on the flight plan — broadcast lengths, YouTube pre-roll, Instagram and TikTok verticals, stills for ads — is shot-listed before the camera rolls, so the assets exist in the edit instead of being faked in the crop.

For your client, that reads as one campaign that feels the same everywhere. For your agency, it's one shoot budget feeding months of channels.
