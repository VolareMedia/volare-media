Why it works

Made for the people
who will watch it.

Comfort

Real people, at ease

Small calm sets and real interview craft. Your team will forget the camera's there.

Nobody on your staff signed up to be on camera, so we don't treat them like talent on a deadline. We keep the crew footprint small, the mood easy, and we interview instead of forcing scripted lines. That's how you get the take where someone says the true thing, plainly.

Every five-star review on our site is from someone we put on camera for the first time.

Batch

One shoot, many videos

A single planned day can yield a training module, a recruiting clip and a quarter of internal content.

The expensive part of video is getting a crew in the building, so once we're there, we plan to capture everything. One well-run day, shot-listed in advance, routinely produces a training module, interview material, a recruiting cut and b-roll your team can reuse for months.

For teams that need video on a rhythm, our ongoing support keeps a video partner on call instead of restarting with a new vendor every quarter.

On site

We come to you

Offices, shops, job sites, a compact crew that works around your operations.

Most corporate video happens on your turf, not a studio. We scout ahead, bring only the gear the job needs, and schedule around your busiest hours. Our crew-first set discipline (clear roles, no chaos) reads as professionalism to your staff and your clients.

Based in Victoria, filming across Vancouver Island, BC and Canada.

Polish

Broadcast-grade finish

The same colour, sound and editing craft we put in TV commercials, in your training library.

Internal doesn't mean second-rate. Your videos get the same editing, colour grading, motion graphics and sound design as our broadcast work, because a polished video says something about your company before anyone speaks.
